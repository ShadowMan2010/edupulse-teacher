EduPulse Parent Notification Options

1. Telegram Bot (RECOMMENDED — completely free)
   - Parents install Telegram and join a bot
   - Bot sends attendance notifications instantly
   - Zero cost, very reliable
   - Setup: 30 min

2. WhatsApp Business API (free up to 1,000 conversations/month)
   - Free for small schools (<50 students)
   - Needs Meta business verification + setup
   - Setup: 1-2 weeks (verification)

3. MSG91 / TextLocal (₹0.20-0.30/SMS)
   - Cheaper SMS option for India
   - Needs DLT registration (govt paperwork)
   - ~₹3,000-4,500/month for 500 daily scans
   - Setup: 1-2 weeks

4. Twilio SMS (~₹0.66/SMS)
   - Most reliable, works globally
   - ~₹10,000/month for 500 daily scans
   - Setup: 1 hour

5. 4G/LTE HAT + SIM Card (one-time hardware cost)
   - Waveshare SIM7600G-H / Sixfab LTE HAT (~$50)
   - Python sends SMS via AT commands over serial
   - Cost: ~₹0.10-0.50 per SMS (depends on SIM plan)
   - No API fees, fully offline
   - Setup: 2-3 hours
